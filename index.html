About SDA Black Market

SDA Black Market is a specialized P2P bridge for the Sidra Chain community. We provide a non-custodial environment where buyers and sellers can exchange SDA for fiat with blockchain-backed security. Our escrow system ensures that SDA is locked in a vault until the seller confirms payment or a dispute is resolved.

Privacy Policy

Your security is our priority. Private keys for your vault and receiving wallet are generated locally in your browser and encrypted. We do not store your keys, PINs, or security answers on our servers. Loss of access credentials means loss of funds. Always keep your security answer safe. We do not track personal identity beyond your mobile number for account routing. You are responsible for backing up your security answer.

Terms & Conditions

1. Commissions: Sellers are charged a 0.5% marketplace fee upon successful release.
2. Withdrawals: A 0.2% fee applies to transfers to external addresses.
3. Compliance: Any attempt to spoof bank proofs or manipulate the system will result in an immediate BAN and vault LOCK.
4. Exact Delivery: Buyers must receive the exact amount of SDA they paid for.
